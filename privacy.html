Your privacy matters to us

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Taalanta Visionaries CBO ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our services.

üìå Key Point: We collect only the information necessary to serve you better and will never sell your personal data to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out volunteer application forms
  • Make donations
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact us via email or phone
  • Participate in our programs or events

This information may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Date of birth (for program participants)
  • National ID number (for verification purposes)
  • Payment information (processed securely through M-Pesa or banks)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device type and operating system

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Program Management: To process volunteer applications, manage program participants, and coordinate activities
  • Communication: To send updates, newsletters, event invitations, and respond to inquiries
  • Donations: To process donations, issue receipts, and provide donor recognition
  • Improvement: To analyze website usage and improve our services
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights
  • Reporting: To create impact reports and share success stories (with permission)

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 We DO Share With:

  • Service Providers: M-Pesa, banks, and email service providers who help us operate (they are bound by confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Partners: With your explicit consent, for collaborative programs

4.2 We DO NOT Share With:

  • Third-party marketers or advertisers
  • Data brokers
  • Anyone for commercial purposes

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption for our website
  • Password-protected databases
  • Limited access to personal data (only authorized staff)
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure payment processing through trusted providers

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Specifically:

  • Volunteer Records: Retained for the duration of your volunteer engagement plus 2 years
  • Donation Records: Retained for 7 years for tax and audit purposes
  • Newsletter Subscribers: Until you unsubscribe
  • Website Analytics: Retained for 26 months (Google Analytics default)

7. Your Rights

Under Kenyan law and international best practices, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another organization
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (e.g., unsubscribe from newsletters)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our programs serve youth aged 16 and above. For participants under 18, we require parental or guardian consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16 without parental consent.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., social media, partner organizations). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

10. International Data Transfers

We are based in Kenya. If you are accessing our website from outside Kenya, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Kenya. By using our services, you consent to such transfer.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification for significant changes (if you're subscribed)

12. Consent

By using our website, registering for programs, or providing your information to us, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
