FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

Do I need prior experience to volunteer?
No prior experience is required! We provide comprehensive training and orientation to all volunteers. What matters most is your passion, commitment, and willingness to learn and contribute to our community.
How much time do I need to commit?
Time commitments vary by position. Most opportunities require 3-10 hours per week. We're flexible and work with your schedule. Some event-based positions have varying time requirements.
Will I receive any training?
Yes! All volunteers attend our orientation session and receive position-specific training. We also provide ongoing support and professional development opportunities throughout your volunteer journey.
Can I volunteer remotely?
Some positions, such as media & communications and fundraising support, can be done remotely. However, many of our programs require on-site presence in Githurai 44 to work directly with the community.
Will I receive a certificate?
Yes! After completing your volunteer commitment, you'll receive a certificate of service and a letter of recommendation upon request. These can be valuable additions to your resume or academic portfolio.
Are there any costs involved?
Volunteering with us is completely free! However, volunteers are responsible for their own transportation to and from volunteer sites. We may provide refreshments during training sessions and events.
Can international volunteers apply?
Yes! We welcome international volunteers who are legally able to volunteer in Kenya. Please ensure you have the appropriate visa and documentation before applying.
How do I track my volunteer hours?
We provide a simple system for tracking volunteer hours. Your coordinator will help you log your time, which is important for certification and recognition purposes.
