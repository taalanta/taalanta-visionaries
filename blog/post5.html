The Githurai Advantage

Training youth in our own community has unique benefits:

  • Local market knowledge - They know what Githurai needs
  • Built-in customer base - Neighbors support neighbors
  • Lower overhead - Work from home/community, no expensive rental space
  • Peer accountability - Community reputation matters
  • Ripple effects - Success stories inspire others

From Training to Thriving: The Journey

Month 1-2: Foundation

  • Assess talents and interests
  • Basic skill training begins
  • Introduction to business principles
  • Immediate income opportunities (recycling, small gigs)

Month 3-4: Building

  • Advanced skill development
  • Business plan creation
  • First clients/customers
  • Mentorship matching

Month 5-6: Launching

  • Independent work begins
  • Marketing and networking
  • Financial management (saving, reinvesting)
  • Problem-solving support

Month 7+: Thriving

  • Established income stream
  • Business growth strategies
  • Peer mentorship (teaching others)
  • Alumni network benefits

Challenges We Help You Navigate

Starting any enterprise in Githurai comes with challenges. We help you tackle:

  • Irregular income - Diversification, savings strategies
  • Skill gaps - Ongoing learning, peer support
  • Confidence issues - Mentorship, small wins, community encouragement
