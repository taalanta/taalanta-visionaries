Our Story

Taalanta Visionaries CBO was founded when Mr. Benedicto Momanyi identified the growing challenges facing youth in the Githurai 44 community. He witnessed young people dropping out of school, falling into drug abuse, and finding themselves on the wrong side of the lawoften engaging in criminal activities that affected both their lives and the community at large.

Driven by a passion to create change, Mr. Ben brought together like-minded individuals to establish an organization that would provide these youth with opportunities, mentorship, and a path toward a brighter future. Today, Taalanta Visionaries continues to transform lives through skills development, job creation, and community integration programs.

Serving Community

Transforming Lives Through Community Action

Located in Githurai 44, Roysambu, Nairobi, Taalanta Visionaries CBO is dedicated to empowering youth, families, and low-income residents. Our comprehensive approach combines talent development, environmental conservation, and sustainable entrepreneurship to create lasting impact.

We believe every young person deserves a chance to succeed, regardless of their past. Our programs specifically target youth who have been released from prison, those struggling with drug abuse, and community members seeking new opportunities for growth.
